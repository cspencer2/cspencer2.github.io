Major

Bachelor of Arts in Technical Communications

I earned a BA in Technical Communications, with a minor in Graphic Design. I enjoyed attending classes covering the wide range of skills necessary for my chosen major to include -- tech writing and editing, grant and proposal writing, graphic design, public relations, and the technology behind the various forms of today's publishing from paper to pixels.

Background Information: where I've been, what I've done

I am a former auto mechanic with thirty years experience. For twenty-five of those years I was a certified ASE Master Technician, and worked on foreign and domestic cars and light trucks. I was involved in all phases of auto repair, but spent the largest portion of my career in electrical diagnosis.

I have been a heavy reader since I was thirteen years old, reading 2 to 3 books a month. This reading across all subject matters ingrained in me the ability and love of writing. Because of my strong interest in producing artwork, I am also attracted to graphic design and enjoy computer aided design projects.

To practice writing to an audience, I started a blog about two years ago and have written approximately 120 articles. My blog is fairly active, and I have many readers that are drawn to my articles with most staying for a friendly conversation. This activity has been a great learning experience, and lets me know if my audience is comprehending the material I am publishing in the way I intended.

Professional Objectives

I wish to pursue a career where I all my abilities in writing, technology, graphic design, and my public relations skills can be utilized to the benefit of my employer to my career satisfaction. I enjoy writing and once I retire I hope to publish creative general interest fiction books and novels, with an office on the beach.
